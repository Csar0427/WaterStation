Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to Zones A, B, and C. These zones cover the main metropolitan area. If you're outside these zones, please contact us for special arrangements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery and GCash payments only. We're working on adding more payment options in the future.

How long does delivery take?

Orders placed before 2PM are typically delivered the same day. Orders placed after 2PM will be delivered the next business day.

Do I need to provide my own containers?

For refill service, yes, you need to provide your own containers. We also offer new containers for purchase if needed.
